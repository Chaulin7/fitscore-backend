Each CV is analysed by a large language model that extracts structured data — skills, experience, education and keywords — and compares them against your job description. Four sub-scores are produced (KW, SK, EX, ED) and combined using the weights you set in the Scoring Weights panel to produce a single Overall Fit score out of 100.

Privacy & Anonymisation

When Anonymise is enabled, names, contact details and other personally identifiable information are stripped from the CV text before it is sent for analysis. No CV data is stored permanently — results are held in your browser session only and cleared when you leave the page.

How Scores Are Calculated

Overview

Each CV is analysed by a large language model that extracts structured information and compares it against your job description across four dimensions.

The Four Sub-scores

  • Keywords (KW) — Key terms from the job description found in the CV.
  • Skills (SK) — Technical and soft skills match between CV and role.
  • Experience (EX) — Seniority, years of experience, and domain relevance.
  • Education (ED) — Degree level, field of study, and certifications.

Overall Score

The four sub-scores are blended using the weights you set in the Scoring Weights panel. Bias toward skills or experience depending on the role.

Score Bands

80+ Strong Fit 50-79 Possible Fit 0-49 Weak Fit

Scores are AI-generated and should be used as a decision aid alongside human judgement.
